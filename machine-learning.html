The point

The point of this page, and the links below it, are twofold. I am taking a machine learning class, and need to take notes. I also want to study web development.

Given training data, find f such that

\hat{Y} \approx f(X)

If the training data are paired X and Y values, then you may do supervised learning. If you have only X, then you are limited to unsupervised learning (basically clustering).
